Client: Franklin H.

Challenge: After a decade in B2B sales at Orange, Franklin wasn't sure if sales aligned with his strengths or interests.

Our Approach:

  • Reframing limiting beliefs
  • Prototyping through informational interviews
  • Building three Odyssey Plans

Outcome: Franklin discovered that he prefers relationship-based, development-oriented work and identified future paths in training, coaching, and customer success.
